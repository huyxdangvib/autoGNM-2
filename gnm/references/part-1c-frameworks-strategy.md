# PART 1c: STRATEGY FRAMEWORK CATALOG

> **TL;DR:** Complete catalog of 128 frameworks across 4 databases. Loaded alongside Part 1b for CREATE tasks. After Part 1b's relevance scoring identifies top categories, consult this catalog to select specific frameworks for Zone 1/2/3 content.


## 1. Strategy Frameworks (51)

### strategic_analysis
- **External SWOT Analysis** — Analyze macro-economic forces, competitive dynamics, industry trends, opportunities and threats
- **Internal SWOT Analysis** — Assess business performance, customer satisfaction, internal processes, organizational capability
- **Five Forces Analysis** (Porter) — Assess supplier/buyer power, rivalry, substitutes, new entrants for structural advantage
- **PESTLE Analysis** — Scan Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental macro factors
- **Market Sizing — TAM SAM SOM** — Size total addressable, serviceable, obtainable market for realistic opportunity grounding
- **Competitive Positioning Map** — Visualize competitive landscape across key dimensions to identify white space
- **Scenario Planning** — Build multiple plausible futures to stress-test strategic choices
- **McKinsey 7S Framework** — Assess alignment: Strategy, Structure, Systems, Style, Staff, Skills, Shared Goals
- **GE Industry Competitiveness** — Evaluate market attractiveness across size, dynamics, competition, profitability
- **GE Corporate Strength** — Evaluate competitive position across share, brand, customer base, infrastructure
- **Amazon Flywheel** — Map self-reinforcing growth loop: experience → traffic → sellers → lower costs → lower prices
- **S-Curve Technology Analysis** — Identify maturity inflection points to time investments

### value_management
- **Deloitte Value Map — Operating Income** — Decompose revenue: volume growth (new sales, retention) + price realization (NIM, fees) + credit risk
- **Deloitte Value Map — Operating Expenses** — Break down costs: customer-facing (marketing, sales) vs non-customer (ops, IT)
- **Deloitte Value Map — Capital** — Analyze balance sheet, RWA, credit/market/operating risk for capital optimization
- **Deloitte Value Map — Expectations** — Manage stakeholder expectations: MIS, financial control, investor relations, regulatory
- **BSC Financial Perspective** — Translate strategy into financials: productivity (cost, asset) + growth (revenue, customer value)
- **BSC Customer Perspective** — Define customer value proposition: attributes, relationships, brand image
- **BSC Internal Process Perspective** — Critical processes: operations, customer management, innovation, risk/regulation
- **BSC Learning and Growth Perspective** — Build capability: human capital, information capital, organizational capital
- **Shareholder Value Tree** — Decompose shareholder value: operating income, expenses, capital, expectations

### governance
- **IBM Direct Layer** — Strategic direction and high-level planning per business domain
- **IBM Control Layer** — Policies, standards, performance management oversight per domain
- **IBM Execute Layer** — Day-to-day operational execution across all eight business domains
- **Three Lines of Defense** — Risk/control structure: business ownership (1st), oversight (2nd), audit (3rd)
- **APQC Mega Process Framework** — Map enterprise activities across 12 process categories
- **PWC Internal Competence Model** — Evaluate: structure, processes, personnel, control systems
- **PWC Barriers to Strategy Execution** — Diagnose failure: understanding, leadership, resource, motivation barriers
- **Balanced Scorecard Strategy Map** — Visualize cause-and-effect across BSC perspectives

### operational_excellence
- **DMAIC — Six Sigma** — Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control cycle to eliminate defects
- **5S Workplace Organization** — Sort, Set-in-order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain
- **5 Why Root Cause Analysis** — Trace problems to root causes by asking why five times
- **BCG Best in Class Operating Model** — Process architecture, technology, org structure, staffing, CX, business case
- **Value Chain Analysis** (Porter) — Map activities from inputs to end customer for value identification
- **Process Excellence — E2E Mapping** — Map end-to-end processes to identify bottlenecks and handoffs
- **SLA and KPI Framework** — Define service levels and KPIs per process step

### customer_management
- **Customer Lifecycle Management** — Manage: awareness, acquisition, development, retention, referral
- **Interbrand Customer Journey** — Map: awareness, inform, attract, welcome, transact, remote touchpoints
- **Customer Segmentation Strategy** — Define segments by needs, behavior, value, risk profile
- **Cross-sell and Up-sell Framework** — Identify product deepening opportunities for wallet share growth
- **Net Promoter System** — Measure loyalty via NPS to diagnose experience quality
- **5C Credit Assessment Framework** — Assess: Character, Capability, Capital, Collateral, Condition
- **Loyalty Economics Framework** (Bain) — Quantify financial value of retention and advocacy

### capability_building
- **Human Capital Strategy** — Recruitment, compensation, training, decision rights for talent base
- **Information Capital Framework** — IT systems, data infrastructure, MIS capabilities
- **Organizational Capital Design** — Culture, governance structure, vision, mission, values
- **HayGroup Leadership Competencies** — Conceptual thinking, influence, team leadership, developing others
- **Digital Transformation Framework** — Reimagine via automation, data, platform technologies
- **Innovation Ambition Matrix** — Balance: core enhancements, adjacent expansions, transformational bets
- **Open Innovation Strategy** — Leverage external ideas, technologies, partners
- **Change Management — ADKAR** (Prosci) — Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
